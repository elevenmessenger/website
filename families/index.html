Eleven for families

Eleven exists because a kid wanted to message their friends and every app said no: wrong device, no phone number, too young, or sign here for ads. Eleven says yes.

Get Eleven Create your space

  • Safe for kids

    A grown-up runs the space and decides who’s in it. No strangers, no feed, no follower counts, nothing built to keep anyone scrolling.

  • No phone number needed

    Kids without SIMs, tablets, hand-me-down iPads. Everyone joins with a link from you.

  • Every device welcome

    iPhone in one pocket, Android in another, a school Chromebook at home. Any browser runs the full app; Mac and iPhone get native ones.

  • Family photos stay in the family

    Photos are encrypted on your device before they leave it, and location data is stripped automatically. We can’t see them. Nobody can.

  • The family logistics

    Shared to-do lists. Reminders that nudge the whole chat — bins out, practice at six. Ask Eleven, the built-in helper, and it sets them up.

  • Grandparent-friendly

    Nothing to install unless they want to, text as big as they need, and read messages stay read on every device.
