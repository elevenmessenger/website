Eleven for communities

Clubs, teams, choirs, committees. One space for the whole community, groups inside it for everything else, and no algorithm between you and your members.

Get Eleven Create your space

  • Open groups

    Members find and join the groups inside your space themselves — match threads, socials, committee. No admin bottleneck.

  • Access levels

    Members, coaches, committee. Some groups open to all, some behind a level. You decide who sees what.

  • Paid memberships

    Paid community? Put a price on an access level and members subscribe, monthly or yearly, on a 90/10 split — 90 to you, 10 keeps Eleven running. Paid into your own Stripe account.

  • One link to join

    Sign-up season is a link in the newsletter. No accounts, no forms, no app-store hunt — any browser works.

  • Helpers on a schedule

    “Remind everyone about training on Thursdays.” Set it once. Done.

  • No algorithm

    Every message reaches every member. Nothing gets ranked, buried, or promoted.
